Internal reference. Each tab traces one thread across all 15 stations, in station-number order.

Act One — The Quest (Stations 1–6): six stations, six virtues, six arches — one letter of D-R-A-G-O-N each, collected out of order (G, R, A, N, D, O at Stations 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6). Station 3 (Salo del Tinell) also pops up all six arch slots in the app, since that room really has six physical arches. Station 7: the group finds the Sword and must arrange the six letters into the right order, pivoting into Act Two — The Hunt (Stations 8–15), where morals shift from virtues to build toward into warnings to watch for.
