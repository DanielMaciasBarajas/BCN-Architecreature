Barcelona Scavenger Hunt — Content Mind Map

Internal reference. Click a station to expand its full content. Use filters to narrow by act / thread; use the panel on the right to jump by theme.

Act One (the Quest, Stations 1–6): six stations, six virtues, six arches — one letter of D-R-A-G-O-N each, collected out of order (G, R, A, N, D, O at Stations 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6). Station 3's room (Saló del Tinell) genuinely has six real arches, so completing it also pops up all six arch slots in the app as a preview. The group carries all six letters forward to Station 7, where they find the Sword and must rearrange the letters correctly to decode DRAGON, pivoting into Act Two (the Hunt, Stations 8–15), where morals shift from virtues to build toward into warnings to watch out for.
